Who I am

Tomomasa received his B.S. and M.E. in system engineering from Aoyama Gakuin University, Tokyo, Japan. He is currently pursuing a PhD in the laboratory of Angela Wang Bo at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), Upper Changi, Singapore. Tomomasa won SUTD PhD Fellowship. His research areas are artificial and augmented intelligence and networked and autonomous systems. During his M.E., He received the Best Student Paper Award from the 8th IIAE International Conference on Industrial Application Engineering.
